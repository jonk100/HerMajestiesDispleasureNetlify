## Day 10 Overview (January 16, 1969)

Tenth day of "Get Back" sessions - **official end of Twickenham sessions.** Beatles restarted rehearsals in new location (Apple Studios, 3 Savile Row) on January 20.

### Context

**January 10:** George Harrison walked out of Get Back sessions at Twickenham
- No longer able to endure growing tensions

**January 12:** Meeting between four Beatles failed to resolve issues
- George left early without committing to rejoining

**January 15:** Four Beatles met again
- George outlined conditions for returning: scrap live TV special, relocate rehearsals from Twickenham to Apple's basement studio (under construction by Magic Alex)
- George agreed to continue being filmed while making new album
- Other Beatles accepted George's terms

### Day 10 Activities

**Official end of Twickenham sessions**

**Set dismantling:**
- Mal Evans, Kevin Harrington, and Glyn Johns dismantled set
- Prepared to relocate equipment to Apple Studios

**Beatles not scheduled to attend**

**Paul McCartney:**
- Came in despite not being scheduled
- Recorded piano demo of "Oh! Darling" (track later released on Abbey Road album)
- **Engineered by:** Glyn Johns
- **Featured in:** Peter Jackson's 2021 "Get Back" documentary

**George Harrison:**
- Visited Twickenham to meet with Glyn Johns
- Together traveled to Apple Corps' headquarters at 3 Savile Row
- Purpose: Inspect basement studio Magic Alex had been constructing

### Magic Alex Studio Inspection

**Discovery:**
- Facilities woefully inadequate
- Plagued by severe levels of distortion and hiss
- Studio unfit for Beatles' needs

**Response:**
- Glyn Johns contacted George Martin for assistance
- **EMI agreed to lend Apple necessary equipment**
- Ensuring band could continue work in functional recording environment

**Installation:**
- Done in following days (January 17-19)
- Beatles restarted rehearsals in new location January 20

### Main Songs Recorded

#### Oh! Darling (Paul)

**This session:**
- Piano demo recorded by Paul
- Engineered by Glyn Johns
- Featured in Peter Jackson's 2021 "Get Back" documentary
- Track later released on Abbey Road album
