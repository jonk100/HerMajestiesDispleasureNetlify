## Day 11 Overview (January 20, 1969)

Eleventh day of "Get Back" sessions - **first day at Apple Studios, 3 Savile Row.** Beatles continued rehearsals following day (January 21).

### Context

**January 10:** George Harrison walked out of Get Back sessions at Twickenham
- No longer able to endure growing tensions

**January 12:** Meeting between four Beatles failed to resolve issues
- George left early without committing to rejoining

**January 15:** Four Beatles met again
- George outlined conditions for returning: scrap live TV special, relocate rehearsals from Twickenham to Apple's basement studio (under construction by Magic Alex)
- George agreed to continue being filmed while making new album
- Other Beatles accepted George's terms

**January 16:** 
- Twickenham set dismantled, equipment relocated to 3 Savile Row
- Magic Alex's basement studio found unfit for Beatles' needs
- Glyn Johns contacted George Martin for assistance
- EMI agreed to provide necessary equipment

**January 17-19:**
- Installation of new EMI equipment at Apple Studios
- Dave Harries, Keith Slaughter, and EMI technical team
- Two portable 4-track mixing consoles combined to create 8-track recording system

### Day 11 Activities

**Studio status:** Still not fully operational

**Beatles activity:**
- Resumed rehearsals
- **No recordings took place**
- **No filming took place**

**Location:** Apple Studios basement, 3 Savile Row, London

### Notes

Minimal information available for this day due to lack of recordings and filming. Day marked transition to new location following Twickenham sessions and Magic Alex studio disaster.

Following day (January 21), rehearsals continued at Apple's basement studio with more substantive activity.
