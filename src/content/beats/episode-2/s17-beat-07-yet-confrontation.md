The final confrontation. Nigel approaches the blackboard question: "BEATLES?"

In small, neat letters under the question, he writes: "NO ACTION REQUIRED."

The institutional conclusion. Case closed.

But when Nigel's back is turned, Clive adds underneath in messier chalk: "YET."

The single word undermines everything. Two versions of reality now exist - Nigel's official record and Clive's private conviction. The bureaucracy has spoken, but the analyst waits.

The chalkboard becomes battlefield of competing authorities. Public conclusion vs private certainty.
