Nigel writes his verdict on the blackboard in small neat letters: 'NO ACTION REQUIRED.' The professional judgment rendered, the institutional position clear.

When his back is turned, Clive adds 'YET' in messier, more urgent chalk. The blackboard now contains the entire series in a single image: institutional caution versus institutional paranoia, the question 'BEATLES?' remaining officially unanswered while both positions are recorded simultaneously.

The operation exists in state of institutional suspension — not escalated, not closed, the question permanently open. Exactly how it will remain until Philip forces the issue.
