Reaches for typewriter again. Hovers. Doesn't type.

Gathers crumpled drafts and smooths one flat. Not to save it - to prove to himself it existed.

Slides persona sheet, atlas, and headline into manila folder. Writes on tab, in block capitals: "FEEDBACK."

Places it beside coat. Ready for morning.

Turns off light.

The typewriter remains loaded. Waiting.

The creative impulse submitted to institutional process. The manufactured outrage campaign delayed by bureaucratic procedure. Clive's individual initiative channeled into organizational approval. The operation ready but waiting for permission.
