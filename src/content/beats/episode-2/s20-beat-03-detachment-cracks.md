Nigel slowly removes the headphones. The professional barrier between observer and observed has been breached.

He checks his watch, then opens a notebook. He does it like a ritual - trying to restore institutional order through procedure.

"SUBJECT: LENNON" - he underlines it, then quickly regrets the drama of the underline and stops.

The words "stories about love" echo in his head. The professional observer is haunted by human truth. He stares at the page for a long moment, the institutional framework suddenly inadequate.

The ritual has failed to restore distance. The human moment has penetrated the professional armor.
