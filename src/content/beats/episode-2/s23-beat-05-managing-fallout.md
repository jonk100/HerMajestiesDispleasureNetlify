He turns to leave, unnoticed by the band.

"Try the seagull one again! Slow it down a bit!" Paul shouts over the noise.

John gives him thumbs-up, face ecstatic.

Brian pauses at door, looks back one last time. They don't need him for this. For the first time, he understands that his job is no longer to build their career, but merely to manage the fallout from their genius.

He quietly closes door, leaving them to their creation. Camera stays inside studio, moving between four of them, tight focused unit, inventing new language together, completely oblivious to world outside - and to man who brought them there, now walking away down long, empty corridor.

The acceptance is complete. The manager has found his new purpose: protecting genius from the world that will inevitably try to destroy it.
