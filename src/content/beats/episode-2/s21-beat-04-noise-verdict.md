"It's just... noise."

The verdict delivered. Philip reduces the sophisticated musical complexity to meaningless noise. The sitar's Eastern tones, the abstract lyrics, the overlapping voices - all collapsed into simple dismissal.

He reaches out and snaps the radio off mid-phrase.

Sudden silence. Restored order. The artistic invasion has been repelled not through understanding but through rejection. Philip has won by refusing to engage.

The military man cannot defeat art through analysis, so he defeats it by declaring it meaningless. The complexity that threatened his orderly world has been simplified into nothingness.
