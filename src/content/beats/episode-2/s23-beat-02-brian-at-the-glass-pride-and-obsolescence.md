Brian watches through the glass as the four-headed creative organism works. The newspaper slips from his folder — 'MORE POPULAR THAN JESUS' half-visible. His expression shows pride mixed with a profound sense of obsolescence.

He came to talk about logistics, but he understands in a single moment that his role has fundamentally changed. He is no longer a builder; he is a damage manager. His job is no longer to build their career but to manage the fallout from their genius.

He closes the door quietly. They don't notice. The camera stays inside the studio as Brian walks away down the long corridor, already managing the crisis that hasn't fully arrived yet.
