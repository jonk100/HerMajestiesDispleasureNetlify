John strums a chord - loud, unresolved. The chord hangs. Neither of them fills the silence.

Then John shrugs and launches into a riff. Paul joins in reluctantly. The music swells - uncertain now, not confident.

The radio in the corner clicks into clearer reception for a moment. A newsreader mentions "a remark by John Lennon" \" and then the signal slips back into static.

Neither of them stop playing.
