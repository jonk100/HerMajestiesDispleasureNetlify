Clive counters Nigel's clinical dismissal with his trademark associative grandeur. 'The medium is the messiah,' he declares, misquoting McLuhan with complete conviction. When Nigel gently corrects — 'I believe it's message' — Clive responds with perfect sincerity: 'Yes. Precisely. That.'

His 'ideological pollen' theory takes shape, a framework designed to convert Nigel's absence of evidence into proof of something far worse. The delusion has its own internal logic, and Clive believes every word.
