The intellectual sparring drops away for one moment of genuine honesty. Nigel names what Clive is really after: 'You want this to matter.'

Clive's response is unexpectedly sincere: 'Someone should be paying attention. Not because he's evil. Because he's believed.' His flash of vulnerability reveals the fear behind the delusion — 'belief is the only thing we don't have a file cabinet for.'

Nigel's 'you're ambitious' comes not as contempt but as compassionate understanding. For one beat, they're not just institutional foils but two men with the same information arriving at different conclusions because they're fundamentally different people.
