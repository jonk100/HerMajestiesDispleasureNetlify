The cavernous storeroom where spy technology comes to die. Fluorescent lights hum over grey metal shelving covered in dust. Percy Dalton, wiry man in brown cardigan, polishes Cold War-era equipment.

Nigel and Clive approach. Nigel carries clipboard with requisition form. Clive vibrates with excitement. The newspaper clipping shows "LENNON QUESTIONS CHRISTIANITY" - the mission's justification kept separate from official paperwork, as if contagious.

Percy looks up with profound, ancient weariness. The institutional gatekeeper has encountered another operational fantasy.

"Form 12-C. In triplicate."

The battle lines drawn immediately. Clive's cultural warfare theory meets Percy's procedural reality. The grand conspiracy begins its collision with mundane bureaucracy.
