Clive's cascade theory reaches its climax - Lennon speaks, girl reads, vicar preaches, order dissolves. "That is a remarkably ambitious bus stop," Nigel notes dryly.

Then the institutional counterattack. Nigel flips to a blank MI5 form. "If you want an escalation, you'll need to fill in an escalation request."

Clive protests: "You can't form your way out of a cultural event."

Nigel's response is devastating: "You can in this building."

The form becomes weapon. Process reasserts itself over theory. Culture must submit to bureaucracy.
