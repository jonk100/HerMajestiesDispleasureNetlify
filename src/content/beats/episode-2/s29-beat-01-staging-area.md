Envelopes stacked by size, carbon paper, battered address book, dog-eared road atlas of United States opened to South.

Clive sits at desk, staring at blank sheet in typewriter. Inserts it. Types: "Dear Sir—"

Stops. Backspaces furiously.

The operational theater ready. The theoretical enthusiasm about to become practical implementation. The cultural warfare machine poised to launch.
