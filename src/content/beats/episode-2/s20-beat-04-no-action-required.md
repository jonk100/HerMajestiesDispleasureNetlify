Nigel continues below the header, writing smaller: "NO ACTION REQUIRED."

He stares at the sentence. The institutional phrase that should provide clarity instead feels like a confession. He taps his pen against the notebook - three taps, stops, three taps, stops. The rhythm of uncertainty.

A young couple walks past, laughing. Nigel watches them go like they're evidence of something he can't file. The world outside his institutional framework continues, filled with ordinary human moments that defy categorization.

He takes the headphones off again, hangs them on the rear-view mirror, and turns the radio on.

The Beatles play. He listens not with suspicion but with something more like doubt. The institutional conclusion has failed to convince its author.
