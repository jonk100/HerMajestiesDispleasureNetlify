The stepladder. John climbing — face alternating between amused and annoyed. The magnifying glass. The tiny word: 'YES.'

John staring at it for a long moment. 'A slow smile of pure relief. In a world that felt increasingly negative and complicated, he found a simple, positive word.'

Their eyes meeting as he climbs down — 'not a look of romance, but of profound, startling recognition.'

John: 'It's the first art show I've been to that hasn't made me want to leave immediately.'

Yoko: 'Art that only says no is not a conversation.'

John finds 'YES' at the top of a ladder. The relief is immediate and genuine. He has found someone who speaks his language — not about fame or art, but about possibility. John has found an alternative to both institutional pressure and fan adoration. Yoko offers something different from both. The word 'YES' becomes a touchstone for the rest of the episode.
