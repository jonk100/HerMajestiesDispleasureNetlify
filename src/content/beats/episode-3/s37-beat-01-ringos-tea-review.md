Ringo entering, radiating good humor. Spotting the teacup: 'Ooh, is that for anyone? Parched, I am.'

Before either can respond — Ringo takes a long, satisfying sip.

Clive going rigid. 'NON-TARGET APPROACHING THE DELIVERY MECHANISM. ABORT! ABORT!' Too late. 'That's nice. Bit lemony. The steam's making lovely patterns.'

Clive's face: confusion, realization, pure silent rage. 'Subject immunity! It's scientifically impossible!' The file: almost entirely blank.

Nigel: 'He's immune, Clive. Some people just are.'

Ringo accidentally intercepts the operation's most sophisticated direct intervention, reviews it as tea, and neutralizes it by being thirsty and finding the tea nice. 'Bit lemony.' The innocence is the immunity. Clive is defeated. The file is almost blank. Nigel reads: 'possesses no exploitable ego or anxieties. Recommendation: ignore.' The immunity is now on the institutional record.
