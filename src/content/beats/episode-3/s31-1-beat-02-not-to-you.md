'Would you say something like that again, Mr. Lennon?'

Beat. 'Not to you.'

The press conference's defining moment. John has performed to managed retraction. He has done what was asked. And then he has answered honestly — refusing not to content but to context. Not 'no I wouldn't say it again.' Not 'yes I would.' 'Not to you.'

The distinction is everything. He will say true things. He will not say them for this. The conference ends with this line hanging. It's not defiance — it's a diagnosis. He won't perform honesty for an audience that wants ammunition.
