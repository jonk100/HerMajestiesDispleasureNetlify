The thread closes here — not in Episode 3 with his death, but here when Paul names what the death meant. 'Daddy's gone away' is the thread's payoff. The thread is closed.

The operation accelerated what was already coming. But George is naming Brian's death as the cause, not as the operation. The institution's invisibility is confirmed — it achieved its result without ever being visible to its targets.
