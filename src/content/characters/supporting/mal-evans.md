# Mal Evans

## Motivations

Mal is devoted to the Beatles and wants to help them succeed however he can. His motivation is service - making sure the practical details are handled so the band can focus on music.

## Internal Conflict

Mal watches the band he's served for years falling apart but can't fix the interpersonal problems. He can handle logistics and equipment but not emotions.

## Character Arc

**Act 2 (1969): The Loyal Witness** - Mal continues his role as road manager and fixer during the Get Back sessions, aware of the tensions but focused on practical matters. He's glad when Billy Preston arrives because "it gave them a lot of life."

## Psychological Profile

**Core Traits:**

- Loyal and devoted
- Practical and logistically minded
- Calm under pressure
- Supportive without being intrusive

**Strengths:**

- Handles all practical arrangements
- Knows the band well enough to anticipate needs
- Maintains stability amid chaos
- Trusted by all four Beatles

## Key Relationships

### The Beatles (The Employers/Friends)

Mal has been with the Beatles since the early days, serving as road manager, equipment handler, and general assistant. He's one of the inner circle who knows them as people, not just as Beatles.

## Historical Timeline Context

**1963-1969:** Mal serves as Beatles road manager and assistant through their most successful years.

**January 1969:** Mal handles logistics for the Get Back sessions, including the planned trip to Africa (cancelled) and later the rooftop concert arrangements.

## Voice and Speech Patterns

**Core Vocal Characteristics:**

- Warm, supportive tone
- Practical and direct about logistics
- Liverpool accent
- Calm and steady

**Typical Phrases:**

- "Now, um, good. So, but just roughly timewise, so I can look into gear..."

## Role in the Story

Mal Evans represents the support staff who keep things running while watching the main story unfold. His practical concerns about timing and equipment contrast with the band's emotional struggles.

## Visual Description

- Mid-thirties, large, friendly presence
- Casual working clothes

- Calm, steady demeanor
- Liverpool working-class background

## Character Function

Mal serves as:
- **The Logistics Handler**: Makes practical arrangements
- **The Loyal Servant**: Devoted to the band's success
- **The Calm Presence**: Stable amid emotional chaos
- **The Witness**: Inner circle observer of the dissolution
