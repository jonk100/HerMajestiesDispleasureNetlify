## Motivations

The Editor represents the media's role in constructing The Beatles' public image, driven by circulation, relevance, and the power to influence cultural conversation.

## Character Arc

**Act 1:** Sensational coverage of Beatlemania and royal controversy  
**Act 2:** More critical reporting as the band becomes more politically and artistically complex  
**Act 3:** Reflective coverage of the band's legacy and cultural significance

## Relationships

- **The Beatles:** Subject of coverage and cultural fascination
- **Maureen Cleave:** Journalist who provides insider access
- **Public:** Audience for media narratives
