---
description: Write dialogue between two or more characters
---

1. analyze and understand the [premise of the screenplay](/home/jk/CreativeWriting/HerMajestiesDispleasure/context/01-Premise.md)
2. identify the characters involved, which should be provided in the prompt
3. analyze and understand the characters involved through their entry in the [characters collection](/home/jk/CreativeWriting/HerMajestiesDispleasure/src/content/characters).
4. analyze and understand any other files linked in the "Related Research" section of the character entry, putting extra emphasis on any research involving quotations
5. analyze the relationships between the characters further, using the [character relationship map](/home/jk/CreativeWriting/HerMajestiesDispleasure/context/03-CharacterMap.json)
6. consider [historical context](/home/jk/CreativeWriting/HerMajestiesDispleasure/context/04-HistoricalContext.md), but give this less weight than the character arcs and motivations.
7. structure of the end document should be the same as [phillip-elizabeth-mbe-return](/home/jk/CreativeWriting/HerMajestiesDispleasure/src/content/dialogue/phillip-elizabeth-mbe-return.md)
8. create the dialogue as a new entry in the [dialogue collection](/home/jk/CreativeWriting/HerMajestiesDispleasure/src/content/dialogue), named appropriately and following the schema outlined for the dialogue collection in the [content collections schema](/home/jk/CreativeWriting/HerMajestiesDispleasure/src/content.config.ts)
