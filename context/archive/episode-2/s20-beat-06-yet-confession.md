Nigel stares at the sentence again. "NO ACTION REQUIRED." It doesn't feel like a conclusion. It feels like a confession.

He taps his pen against the notebook a few more times before adding: "YET."

The surrender. Clive's theoretical pollen has infected the institutional man. The phrase that Clive wrote secretly under Nigel's official conclusion on the blackboard now appears in Nigel's private notebook.

The contagion is complete. The procedural man has been converted to the analytical man. The institutional certainty has been replaced by institutional anxiety.

Outside, somewhere beyond the rain, the song reaches its chorus. Nigel sits very still, listening as if the music might confess to something. It doesn't. That's the problem.
