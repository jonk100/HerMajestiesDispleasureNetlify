"Pah! Zat is commerce... for zee little girls. I speak of zee noise... zee feedback. The screaming of the guitar."

John's mouth twitches. Laugh he refuses to give. "And what's that get you, then? Apart from tinnitus."

Clive leans in, conspiratorial. "The others… the cute one, the quiet one, the drummer… Zay are anchors. Zay.. drag you down into the ocean of mediocrity."

John narrows eyes. He doesn't object. He considers.

"Bit harsh on Ringo."

"You are a revolutionary, ja? An iconoclast! Why do you sing of holding hands? You should sing of tearing down the structures. You should destroy the song from the inside."

The psychological operation begins. Klaus validates John's artistic frustration while positioning bandmates as limitation rather than collaborators.
