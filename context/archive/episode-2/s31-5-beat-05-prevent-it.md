"Sir, with respect, what exactly does 'intervene' mean in practical terms?" Nigel asks.

Sir Derek smiles. Not pleasant sight. "It means you stop being observers and start being active participants in the defense of the realm. It means you find out what they're planning next. It means you prevent it."

He walks to easel, studies album cover one more time. "And for God's sake, find out why there's a hand coming out of Paul McCartney's head holding a garden gnome. That can't be good for anyone."

The institutional mandate delivered. Operation Minuet transformed from surveillance to active prevention. The cultural security operation becomes censorship mission. The Beatles' artistic evolution now threat to be neutralized.

The garden gnome mystery becomes final symbol of institutional incomprehension facing artistic revolution.
